About me

Pablo Picasso (1881-1973) was a Spanish artist and one of the most influential figures in 20th-century art. He co-founded Cubism and created revolutionary works like Les Demoiselles d'Avignon and Guernica. Picasso worked in various styles, including the Blue and Rose periods, and experimented with sculpture, ceramics, and printmaking. His diverse and prolific career left a lasting impact on modern art.
